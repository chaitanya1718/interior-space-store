Product Details keyboard_arrow_down

Designed by renowned architect Studio K, the Kensho chair challenges the boundary between furniture and sculpture. Every joint is meticulously crafted using traditional techniques, eliminating the need for visible hardware.

The cushions are filled with a high-density, eco-friendly foam wrapped in down for a balance of structure and softness.

Materials & Care keyboard_arrow_down

Frame: Solid FSC-certified Ash wood with a zero-VOC matte finish.

Upholstery: 100% Belgian Linen. Removable covers for easy dry cleaning.

Care: Dust wood frame with a soft, dry cloth. Avoid direct sunlight to prevent fading. Vacuum upholstery regularly using an upholstery attachment.
